My name is Brendon Delate and I am part software developer, part entrepreneur.

I wrote my first line of code when I was 15 and was immediately hooked. Once the bug had bitten, I spent high school coding in Basic, then Turbo Pascal and finally Visual Basic.

In university I earned a Bachelor of IT (majoring in Software Development), during which time I obtained 7 distinctions and was placed on the Deans list. While I touched on several languages during this time, my main focus was Java.

Following university I worked as an IT focused Business Analyst for 8 years. During this time I gained insight into the software development process both from the business as well the developers perspective.

Throughout those years, my goal was always to start a company by leveraging the skills I had gained along with my passion for programming and the stock market.

After 8 years I left my Business Analyst career to create Diversify Portfolio which is a subscription based website allowing customers to analyse their stock portfolios and look for opportunities.

More detail on Diversify Portfolio and the technology behind it can be found here.

My current focus from a programming perspective is on full stack development centered around Python, Django, Rest API's and ReactJS.
